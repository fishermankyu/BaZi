# 従格 (Following Structures) — When the Ordinary Strength Formula Doesn't Apply

Only relevant when Score% (from `strength_formula.md`) falls past the 极强 (>75%) or 极弱 (<25%) extremes. In the 35–75% range, don't check 従格 — the ordinary 扶抑 (support/drain) reading applies directly.

**School disagreement, flag this to the user if asked**: strict traditional 子平法 purists hold that 従格 only has two real members — 従财格 and 従杀格 — and dismiss 従儿格/従強格/従弱格/従旺格 as later "旺衰派" additions patched on to explain charts the older system couldn't classify. In practice, the fuller taxonomy below is what most modern teaching material and practitioners use, so this skill covers all of it — but say so if a user pushes on "is this really traditional," rather than presenting it as uncontested.

## Common prerequisites (all 従格 types need these before checking type-specific rules)

1. **月令本气 must be the very thing being followed**, and it must be transparent on a heavenly stem somewhere in the chart (a hidden-only month qi that never surfaces on a stem doesn't qualify as "得用").
2. **Day Master must be essentially rootless** — no 印/比劫 in the branches, or if a trace exists, it must be fully overcome (阳克阳 / 阴克阴 required for a clean kill; 阴克阳 doesn't fully suppress and disqualifies 真従, falling back to 假従 or ordinary weak/strong reading).
3. **阴干 follows more easily than 阳干** — a yin Day Master with thin qi surrenders more completely; a yang Day Master with even a small root tends toward 假従 (fake/impure following) rather than 真従 (true following). If constructing test charts, prefer a yin Day Master for cleaner 従弱 cases.
4. **The whole chart's qi must be pure and flowing toward the followed element** — scattered opposition (even small amounts of the "wrong" element) disqualifies true 従格; fall back to ordinary 身弱/身强 with a note that 従格 was considered and rejected.

## 従弱系 (extremely weak Day Master, follows the dominant opposing force)

| Type | Month branch requirement | Extra condition | Avoid (忌) | Favors (喜) |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| **従财格** | Month branch = 财 | Heavenly stems need 食伤 present (gives the 财 a generating source) | 印 (protects Day Master, breaks the structure), 比劫 | 财, 食伤 |
| **従杀格 / 従官格** | Month branch = 官杀 | Heavenly stems need something that controls/drains the Day Master present | 食伤 (attacks/controls the 杀, works against the following) , 印比劫 | 官杀, 财 (feeds the 杀) |
| **従儿格 (従食伤格)** | Month branch = 食伤 | **Exception**: tolerates the Day Master having some root, as long as root strength stays below self-sufficiency — unlike 従财/従杀 which require near-zero root | 印 (directly attacks 食伤 — the single worst enemy of this structure) | 食伤, 财 (食伤生财, "儿又生儿"), **and unusually, 比劫 is NOT avoided** — 比劫 only feeds 食伤 rather than opposing the following, so it's tolerated or even welcomed |

## 従強系 (extremely strong Day Master, follows its own kind)

| Type | Characteristic | Avoid (忌) | Favors (喜) |
|---|---|---|---|
| **従旺格** | 比劫 outweighs 印; 官杀 nearly absent or fully neutralized/combined away | 官杀 (triggers disaster), 财 (比劫争财, seriously inauspicious) | 比劫, 印; 食伤 is tolerable (not avoided) if 印 is light and 比劫 heavy — treated as 泄秀 rather than opposition |
| **従強格 (従印格)** | 印 outweighs 比劫 | 食伤 (directly opposes 印) | 比劫, 印; **unlike 従旺格, does NOT avoid 官杀** — 官杀 generates 印 here rather than opposing the structure |
| **専旺格** (曲直/炎上/稼穑/従革/润下 — one for each of the five elements) | Stricter than 従旺格: requires a *structural* pure combination in the branches (a full directional set or 三会), not just a majority of 比劫. E.g. 甲/乙 Day Master with 寅卯辰 all present in the branches = 曲直格. | **官杀 is the single worst enemy** — triggers severe 破格, traditionally associated with serious setbacks (loss of position, wealth, family conflict, even worse in older texts) | Same element and what generates it (印) |

## Integrating with the Score% workflow

Only run this check when Score% is past 75%/25%. Sequence:

1. Check whether the month branch's main qi matches the direction implied by the extreme (従弱 needs month branch = 财/官杀/食伤; 従強 needs month branch = same element as Day Master or 印).
2. Check whether that month branch main qi is transparent on a heavenly stem.
3. Check whether the Day Master's root is genuinely cleared out (or, for 従儿格 specifically, within the tolerated small-root exception).
4. Check overall purity — any significant scattered opposition disqualifies true 従格. If disqualified, say so explicitly and fall back to the ordinary 极强/极弱 reading from `strength_formula.md`, noting 従格 was considered.
5. If a 従格 does qualify, name the specific subtype (not just "従格" generically) and give its favor/avoid list — this inverts or modifies the standard 印比劫 vs 官杀财食伤 heuristic from `strength_formula.md`, so don't apply that heuristic once a 従格 is confirmed.

**Caveat to give the user**: 従格 is one of the more contested and easy-to-overclaim areas of BaZi — cite one source's warning that many practitioners mislabel ordinary weak/strong charts as 従格 because it has more prestige/drama attached, when the strict conditions above (especially "purity" and "root fully cleared") often aren't really met. Default to skepticism: an extreme Score% is necessary but not sufficient for 従格, and most 75%+/25%- charts will still fail one of the purity or transparency conditions and should be read as ordinary 极强/极弱 instead.
